# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior:**
- Being respectful and constructive
- Accepting feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the project
- Showing empathy toward others

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces — issues, pull requests, wiki, and any other communication channels.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive behavior may be reported to the project maintainer at [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/B2JK-Industry/Agent_Life_Space/issues). All complaints will be reviewed. The maintainer reserves the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.
